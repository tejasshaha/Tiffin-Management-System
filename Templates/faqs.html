FAQS

How does this concept work?

Yummy Tiffins is India’s first customized online tiffin service. Follow the following simple steps to join yummy tiffins.

1. Sign up/ Register (Please fill detailed address with pincode and landmark)

2. You can recharge your wallet via debit card/ credit card

3. Choose customized meal


Do I need to log in every day on the website to place an order?

You need to login everyday to select your meal as menu get updated every day


Is there any minimum monthly/weekly commitment?

There is no minimum monthly/ weekly commitment to order meals from us.

You can place orders as and when you want.


How much is the delivery charge?

There is no delivery charges.It is absolutely free :)


How do I check my order history?

You can always check your history by visiting view details on your dashboard and just you have to enter date for which you want to see your details.


How do I do if order does not get delivered to me?

At Happy Tiffins, we put in a lot of effort to ensure that your order reaches you on time and in an ideal condition. However, due to unforeseen events your order may reach you late and in rare cases may be undelivered. In such a circumstance, please contact the delivery guys or contact us. We assure you that every attempt will be made to rectify the situation or compensate you.
